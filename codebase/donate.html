Since NOAF began distributing pledges, we have funded over 800 clients.

In the last fiscal year, our average pledge to patients was $120. This assistance offsets not only the cost of an abortion -- in Louisiana, the average medical abortion in Louisiana costs $500 -- which does not include lost wages from time off work, etc.

Why should I donate?

Our clients represent the diversity of our community. The average client age is 27, and the age range is 14-43. More than two-thirds of the women we serve are mothers (68.6%), and 82% of our clients are religious.

Please consider taking your support to the next level with a convenient, one-time or monthly gift. Donors ensure our essential work is sustained year-round and we’re able to support our callers to access quality healthcare.

The New Orleans Abortion Fund an all-volunteer 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization and all gifts are tax-deductible.
