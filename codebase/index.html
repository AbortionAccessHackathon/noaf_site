We’re Here to Help

We affirm a woman's right to control her body and her destiny, and work to ensure that all people have access to quality medical care, regardless of their economic situation.

Working with local medical providers, we provide compassionate and empowering assistance to women seeking abortions who are unable to fully fund their abortion, and distribute pledges as available.

We help fill in the gap between what patients can afford and what they need to pay.

Here’s How to Support

We’re on the front lines fighting for reproductive justice for women. From volunteering to donating, there are various ways you can join us and defend Louisiana’s reproductive freedom.

As well, take a look at the work being done at Expose Fake Clinics to see how you can help protect abortion access across the country.
