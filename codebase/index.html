We’re Here to Help

After receiving an ultrasound from NOLA clinics and making up their minds to obtain an abortion, we help fill in the gap between what patients can afford and what they need to pay.

We affirm a woman's right to control her body and her destiny, and work to ensure that all people have access to quality medical care, regardless of their economic situation.
